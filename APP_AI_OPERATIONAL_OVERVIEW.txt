# Flowr AI Operational Overview

## 1. System Architecture
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router).
- **Router Logic**: Intent-based switching matrix.
- **Provider Registry**: Supabase `router_chains` table (platform: 'app' | 'telegram').
- **Local Fallback**: `src/data/store.constants.ts`.

## 2. Intent Categories
- `TOOL_CALLING`: Workspace actions & complex task execution.
- `WEB_SEARCH`: Real-time information with search grounding.
- `COMPLEX_THINKING`: High-depth reasoning.
- `MEDIUM_THINKING`: Balanced general tasks.
- `FAST_SIMPLE`: Sub-second response latency.
- `IMAGE_GEN`: High-fidelity creative generation.
- `AUDIO_VOICE`: Native voice dialog & transcription.

## 3. Current Router Configuration (v1.7)
Applied: 2026-04-21
Platform: Web App

### Primary Models
- **Tools/Reasoning**: Llama 3.3 70B (Groq) & Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- **Search**: Google Search Grounding.
- **Fast**: Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite.
- **Voice**: Gemini 3 Flash Live.
- **Images**: Cloudflare Workers AI.

## 4. Capacity Management
- **Groq**: Llama 3.3 70B (1,000 RPD), Llama 3.1 8B (14,400 RPD).
- **Google**: Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite (500 RPD), Search Grounding (1,500 RPD).
- **Cloudflare**: 100,000 RPD shared quota.
