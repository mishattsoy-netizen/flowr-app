# Home Page (`/`) — most complex page

One scroll story: **calm → problem → product → magic → trust → download.** Nine beats. Two interactive moments. Copy below is directional — tone locked, exact words polishable.

---

## Beat 1 — Hero

Near-empty warm charcoal viewport. **The Current** (Flow Line) draws itself across the screen (~2s) behind the statement. The one orange moment: the filled shape inside the illustration.

> # Find your flowstate.
> Notes, canvas, tasks, and a personal AI — one quiet app instead of five loud ones.

- **Download free** (primary, brand blue)
- **Meet Flowr AI** (ghost, mono) → `/ai`
- Small print, DM Mono: `macOS · Windows · Free · Beta`

## Beat 2 — The problem strip

Editorial serif passage, narrow measure (~720px), almost no imagery. Names the enemy:

> Your notes live in one app. Your whiteboard in another. Your tasks in a third. Your AI doesn't know any of them exist. You spend the day alt-tabbing between tools that don't talk to each other.
>
> **Flowr is what happens when they do.**

## Beat 3 — The workspace tour

Three alternating feature blocks (screenshot left / copy right, then flipped). Real app captures in floating glass frames; short silent videos where motion matters; one spot-doodle per block in the margin.

1. **Notes** (S1, V2) — *"Write like it's paper, structure like it's a database."* Block editor, slash commands, tables, columns, media.
2. **Canvas** (S2, V1) — *"Think in space, not just in lines."* Infinite whiteboard: moodboards, diagrams, graphs; arrows that stick to shapes.
3. **Tasks** (S3, V3) — *"Every workspace gets its own board."* Kanban tracker with priorities, subtasks, due dates — no separate task app.

Optionally a fourth quiet card for the dashboard (S4) if design wants a 2×2.

## Beat 4 — Interactive moment: the mini canvas

Bounded playground strip. Visitors can drag 2–3 shapes, draw an arrow that sticks, scribble freehand. One caption:

> *Go on, drag something.*

Proves the product's feel instead of claiming it. Uses real canvas rendering (adapted from app code).

## Beat 5 — The AI turn

Tone shifts slightly darker/quieter — the reveal that the app has a soul. A **real Brain graph** (app UI, not illustration) grows on scroll-in: nodes appear, connections draw.

> ## Meet Flowr AI. It has a Brain — and you're the one who trains it.
> Feed it your notes. Give it rules. Or just tell it what you need — it compiles its own Brain, remembers, briefs you, and keeps your workspace in order.

Then a **simulated chat exchange** (auto-playing, ~3 turns — student example reads best visually; include the AI creating a note, not just chatting).

Button: **See how the Brain works →** `/ai` (mono; blue stays with nav's Download).

## Beat 6 — Three lives, one app

Persona vignettes as three editorial cards, each a 3-step story:

- **The Freelancer** — keeps every client in its own workspace → asks the Brain to brief her each morning on deadlines across all of them → Flowr AI knows which client meant "the usual rate."
- **The Founder** — dumps meeting notes, decks, half-ideas → the Brain builds a company memory → drafts investor updates from what actually happened this month.
- **The Student** — stores essays, homework, test dates, study materials in notes attached to the Brain → sets a rule: "help me with uni" → gets a tutor that already knows the syllabus.

## Beat 7 — Yours, locally

Trust beat. Serif statement + short copy:

> **Your second brain shouldn't live on someone else's computer.**
> The free app is local-first — everything stays in a local database on your device. No account required. Export any note to Markdown whenever you like. Cloud sync exists when you want it — and it's opt-in, not default.

## Beat 8 — Built in the open

Small beat: the three latest changelog entries (real, dated, DM Mono versions, notable ones signed — Mikhail) + one line:

> Flowr is built in the open. Users vote on what it learns next. **See the roadmap →** `/updates`

## Beat 9 — Final CTA

Nearly empty viewport mirroring the hero. The Flow Line returns and settles into a resting shape.

> ## Ready when you are.
> **Download free** (blue)
