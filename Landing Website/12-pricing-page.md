- **What happens to my data if I never pay?** Nothing. It's in a local database on your device, and you can export notes to Markdown anytime. It's yours.
- **What does "generous storage" mean?** Exact numbers announced at Pro launch; generous means you won't think about it.
- **When do Pro and Max launch?** When they're good. Leave your email — you'll know first.
- **Do beta users get anything?** Yes — an early-supporter discount on your first period of Pro.
- **Can I use Flowr on my phone?** The web app (Pro) works in mobile browsers; native apps are a roadmap topic — vote on it.
- **Do I need an account for the free app?** No.
