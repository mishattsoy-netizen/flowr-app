# Getting Started Page (`/start`)

A slim, scannable single page — NOT a docs site. Readable in three minutes. Job: get a fresh installer from "opened the app" to "made something" fast, and plant the Pro seed at the end.

---

## Header

> # The three-minute tour.
> You've installed Flowr. Here's everything you need to feel at home.

## Five steps (numbered serif headings, one screenshot each)

**01 — Make a workspace.**
Workspaces keep areas of life separate — clients, projects, school. Create one, name it, done.

**02 — Write your first note.**
Type `/` for anything: headings, checklists, tables, columns, images. (Screenshot: S1 — slash menu open)

**03 — Think on the canvas.**
Open a canvas, drop shapes and text, connect them with arrows that stick. Moodboards, diagrams, graphs — space to think. (Screenshot: S2)

**04 — Set your tasks.**
Every workspace has a board. Drag tasks between Todo, In Progress, Today. Priorities, subtasks, due dates. (Screenshot: S3)

**05 — Arrange your dashboard.**
Widgets in a bento grid — clock, calendar, recent files, tasks. Drag, resize, make it yours. (Screenshot: S4)
