## 2. The mechanic — three serif-numbered editorial steps

**01 — Just talk.**
Tell Flowr AI what you need: *"Set up a brain for my freelance work."* It compiles the Brain for you — attaches the right notes, drafts the rules, organizes memories. You never have to wire anything by hand.

**02 — Or take the wheel.**
Everything the AI builds is visible and editable. Attach notes yourself, rewrite rules in plain language ("act as my manager," "brief me every morning"), inspect and delete memories. The Brain is glass, not a black box.

**03 — Let it work.**
Morning briefs, analysis reports, workspace sorting, notes written for you — with your full context behind every answer. It builds memories and a profile of how you work as it goes.

Framing to preserve: **"the AI compiles the Brain — you keep the keys."** Easiest onboarding story and trust story in one.
