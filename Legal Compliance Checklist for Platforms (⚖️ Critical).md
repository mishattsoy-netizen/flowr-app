## 4 Non-Negotiable Legal Requirements
### 1️⃣ Privacy Policy
**Risk:** Collecting emails or user data without a privacy policy is illegal in the EU. **GDPR fines: up to €20 million**
- Required if you collect ANY user data (emails, analytics, user behavior, etc.)
- Must be public and easily accessible
- Must disclose what data you collect, why, and how you store/use it
- Non-negotiable in EU; best practice everywhere
---
### 2️⃣ Cookie Consent
**Risk:** Loading Google Analytics or ANY tracker without explicit user consent violates EU law in every country
- User must opt-in BEFORE you load trackers/analytics
- "Consent first" banner required
- Applies to: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Segment, Hotjar, etc.
- Affects all EU users regardless of where your company is based
---
### 3️⃣ Terms of Service
**Risk:** No T&S = zero legal protection if a user damages your platform or you need to ban them
- Required to ban/restrict users without legal liability
- Protects against abuse (spam, fraud, DMCA takedowns)
- Defines what users can/can't do on your platform
- Essential for any user-facing service
---
### 4️⃣ Exposed User Data
**Risk:** Database leaks, misconfigured cloud storage, or unsecured APIs exposing user data = massive liability. **GDPR fines: up to €20M + class-action lawsuits**
- Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit
- Use strong authentication (OAuth, MFA)
- Audit database access logs regularly
- Never log passwords or payment card data
- Have an incident response plan
- Notify users within 72 hours of a breach (EU law)
---
## Bottom Line
These four aren't optional if you:
- Operate in the EU or have EU users
- Collect any user data (even just emails)
- Have a community/multiple users
- Want legal protection
