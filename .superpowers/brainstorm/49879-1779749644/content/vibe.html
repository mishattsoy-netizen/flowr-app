What vibe should the welcome page have?

Your friends are opening Flowr for the first time — what should they feel?

🤗

Warm & Personal

Feels like a handwritten note. "Hey, you made it!" energy. Uses the person's name if possible. Cozy, friendly, intimate.

✨

Premium & Exclusive

Feels like you got a VIP pass. Dark, polished, confident. "You're one of the chosen few." Sophisticated tone.

🎉

Playful & Celebratory

Confetti, animation, a little party. "You're in!" Fun, energetic, makes you smile. More dopamine hit than sophistication.
