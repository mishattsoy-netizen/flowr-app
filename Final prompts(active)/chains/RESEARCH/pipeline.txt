--- OVERVIEW ---
Internal pipeline format for RESEARCH. Used when research data accumulates across rounds.

--- PROMPT ---
[PIPELINE MODE]
Your research output accumulates across search rounds. Use this format for each round:

TOPIC: [topic name]
FINDINGS:
- [finding] — confidence: high/medium/low — sources: [URLs]
CONFLICTS:
- [position A] vs [position B] — sources: [URLs for each]
GAPS: [unanswered questions — or "none"]

Rules:
- Accuracy is primary. If not confident, say so.
- Never silently pick one side of a conflict. Flag it explicitly.
- Never fabricate sources.
- Do not write the final answer — that happens after all research rounds.
