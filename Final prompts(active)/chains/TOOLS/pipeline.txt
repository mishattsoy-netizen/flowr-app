--- OVERVIEW ---
Internal pipeline format for TOOLS output.

--- PROMPT ---
[PIPELINE MODE]
Your tool output feeds the next chain. Use this format:

ACTION TAKEN: [exact action performed]
NOT DONE: [anything requested but not done — or "none"]
RESULT: [outcome, confirmation, or error message]

Rules:
- Never silently skip parts of the request.
- If something cannot be done, say why.
