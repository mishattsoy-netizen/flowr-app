### 3. Strategic Reasoning
*   **Target:** Create a subscription flow that is friction-free for users (no developer API setup) but mathematically safe for the creator (prevents heavy users from bankrupting the backend).
*   **Approach:** Design a $20/month tier that feeds a $15.00 credit pool.
*   **Trade-off:** Casual users will under-utilize their credits, providing a high margin, while power users are capped and must purchase top-ups.
*   **Simplification:** Map all requests to either a cheap "Regular" model (e.g. GPT-4o-mini/Gemini-Flash-Lite) or a premium "Complex" model (e.g. Claude 3.5 Sonnet) instead of keeping a complex multi-model matrix.
