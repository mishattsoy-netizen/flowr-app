### 2. Objective Reconstruction
Implement the previously approved plan to fix three UI/UX issues in the chat:
1. Remove the delay when switching from an empty "new chat" state to an active chat. Make the transition instant.
2. Fix the streaming animation on AI responses which were appearing instantly instead of typing out.
3. Use a Link icon as a fallback while the favicon for source link pills is loading.

### 3. Strategic Reasoning
- **Delay fix:** To prevent layout shifts and delays caused by unmounting/remounting complex components, the separate `AIAssistant` component in the center of the screen was removed. Now, a single `AIAssistant` in `ChatPage.tsx` snaps instantly from the center (using `top-1/2 -translate-y-1/2`) to the bottom (`bottom-0`) without animating, providing the instant transition requested.
- **Streaming fix:** The `useWordReveal` hook was initialized with `initialProgress: 'complete'`, preventing the typing animation. By changing it to `'zero'`, we restore the smooth typing effect for new incoming chunks.
- **Link Icon fix:** Favicons load asynchronously, causing source pills to initially render empty. A `<LinkIcon>` from `lucide-react` is now rendered underneath the `<img>` tag to provide an immediate placeholder icon. If the favicon fails to load, an `onError` handler hides the broken image icon, seamlessly exposing the fallback Link icon.
